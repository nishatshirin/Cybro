How It Works
Who We Are

A hybrid stock photography website that allows amateur and semi-professional photographers to showcase their work and present them for sale. We are a community based website focused on helping local photographers. We are Indian and source photographs from Indian photographers.

How to Register

During the process of Registration, if you are a photographer (you will be prompted with this question), you will be required to fill a bio page about yourself and you may choose to check mark questions that are relevant to you, along with the Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy. Please know that filling up this bio form to the maximum you can, is recommended.

5
 
How to Register

In the Menu bar on the top left there is a link that says "Contribute." View the Sign In and Sign Out options. If you are new to the site, please do click on Sign Up and follow a procedure to get yourself registered on the site. You will need an email address that you use frequently. There is also a link for Forgot Password.

4
Upload Your Photographs

After signing in, in the Menu bar, click on Gallery. A Gallery is a collection of photographs that has been submitted for various Genres. - "+ Gallery" is used to add a new collection of photographs- My Galleries shows what has already been submitted to the Administrator. These are editable. - Add Gallery and + Gallery do essentially the same thing.

6
 
genres

Genres are broad classifications for various forms of photography, also known as categories. For example, Nature photography overlaps the fields of -- and is sometimes considered an overarching category including -- wildlife photography, landscape photography, and garden photography. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nature_photography

7
View And Edit Approved Photographs

Every photograph submitted to PhotoWoto is screened to meet standards set down by our team of curators. You will receive an email stating the pictures that were chosen from the selection you presented. You have the option to edit the information for each photograph. These will be viewable by other users in each Photo Details Page navigable via the arrow zoom icon in response to a mouseover on the representative image.

8
Add or Remove Photographs

Using the Gallery feature, you may add or remove pictures from your album. All additions are subject to approval and the consequent process which follows. See also item 6. You may add images to a Gallery of less than 10 photographs but cannot do so if there are 10 photographs to begin with (previously added by you)
